Papa John’s Outlet Interior Design by Kenneth Designs

Kenneth Designs collaborated with Papa John’s to create a modern and inviting interior for one of their flagship outlets. The design concept focused on reflecting the brand’s core values—freshness, quality, and simplicity—through clean lines, functional layouts, and contemporary aesthetics. Incorporating a bright, open space with minimalistic decor, Kenneth Designs used a palette of neutral tones with pops of brand colors to create an energetic yet relaxing dining environment. The seating arrangement balances comfort and functionality, optimizing the space for both dine-in and take-out customers.

Signature design elements like the custom lighting fixtures and sleek wooden furniture enhance the modern feel of the restaurant, while the tiled walls, featuring playful patterns, echo Papa John’s vibrant brand identity. Every detail, from furniture placement to lighting, was meticulously planned to ensure a seamless flow for both customers and staff, fostering a welcoming atmosphere that aligns with Papa John’s commitment to quality.

This project highlights Kenneth Designs’ ability to craft spaces that are not only aesthetically pleasing but also cater to the specific functional needs of a global brand like Papa John’s, ensuring the space serves as a perfect backdrop for their "Better Ingredients, Better Pizza" ethos.
