work Process

Our Approach to
Interior Excellence

Concept & Brief

01

We begin by understanding your vision and gathering requirements to create a comprehensive design brief. This includes understanding your lifestyle, aesthetic preferences, and functional needs.

Space Planning

02

Next, we map out your space with precise measurements and layout options to maximize functionality and aesthetics. This phase includes floor plans, room configurations, and furniture placements.

Design & Visuals

03

Here, we translate concepts into 3D models and visualizations. You'll see detailed renderings, mood boards, and color palettes to help you envision your space before any work begins.

Material Selection

04

We guide you through the process of selecting materials, finishes, fixtures, and furnishings, ensuring every element aligns with your style and budget.

Execution & Supervision

05

With design finalized, our team coordinates and oversees the execution phase. From managing contractors to ensuring quality control, we handle all aspects of the project timeline.

Installation & Styling

06

In the final phase, we install furniture, décor, and fixtures to bring your design to life. Every detail is meticulously arranged, and we walk you through your newly transformed space.
